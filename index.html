These experiences shaped my approach to challenges, instilling resilience and unwavering dedication to mission success.

Post-Military Career

After concluding my service with the US Army in April 2014, I embarked on a multifaceted career journey that paved my path to the tech industry. Initially, I transitioned into the construction sector, where I developed foundational skills in project management, teamwork, and safety protocols at Ascend Roofing Company LLC (2017 - August 2018) and Stoneway Roofing Supply (2018 - 2019). This period was instrumental in enhancing my capabilities in managing complex projects and ensuring workplace safety. In September 2022, I shifted focus to a role that leveraged my expertise in crisis intervention and advocacy as a Case Manager for Mason County, WA. Until January 2023, I navigated the intricacies of the court system, providing vital support and guidance to clients. This experience not only deepened my skills in communication and crisis management but also underscored the importance of empathy and effective problem-solving in high-stakes environments. Seeking to integrate my diverse experiences with a burgeoning interest in technology, I then pursued independent projects as a self-employed professional from January 2023 to November 2023. During this time, I delved into IT consulting and project management, focusing on how technology can solve real-world problems and enhance community engagement. This phase marked a significant turn in my career, laying the groundwork for my full immersion into web development. Driven by a passion for technology and its transformative potential, I am now fully committed to mastering web development. My journey is characterized by continuous learning and application, from foundational web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to exploring the vast possibilities of backend development and AI. My diverse background, from the military to case management and now tech, has equipped me with a unique perspective and a versatile skill set that I am eager to leverage in the field of web development.
